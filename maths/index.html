Key Relationships

  • sec θ = 1/cos θ
  • cosec θ = 1/sin θ
  • cot θ = 1/tan θ = cos θ/sin θ
  • tan θ = sin θ/cos θ
  • sin²θ + cos²θ = 1
  • 1 + tan²θ = sec²θ
  • 1 + cot²θ = cosec²θ
