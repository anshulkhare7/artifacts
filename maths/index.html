Key Relationships

Reciprocal Identities

  • sec θ = 1/cos θ
  • cosec θ = 1/sin θ
  • cot θ = 1/tan θ

Quotient Identities

  • tan θ = sin θ/cos θ
  • cot θ = cos θ/sin θ

Pythagorean Identities

  • sin²θ + cos²θ = 1
  • 1 + tan²θ = sec²θ
  • 1 + cot²θ = cosec²θ

ASTC Mnemonic - Quadrant Signs

"All Students Take Calculus" - Remembering which trig functions are positive in each quadrant:

  • Quadrant I (0° to 90°): All - All trig functions are positive (sin, cos, tan)
  • Quadrant II (90° to 180°): Students - Only Sine (and cosecant) is positive
  • Quadrant III (180° to 270°): Take - Only Tangent (and cotangent) is positive
  • Quadrant IV (270° to 360°): Calculus - Only Cosine (and secant) is positive
